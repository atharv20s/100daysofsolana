Just wrapped up Week 1 of the #100DaysOfSolana challenge! 🚀

I generated my first Solana keypair using the CLI and connected a custom browser wallet dashboard to devnet. The biggest shift from Web2: my identity is just a cryptographic keypair (Base58 public key) with no central database, usernames, or password resets. 

Next up: writing our first program/understanding accounts. Let's go! 🤝 @solana_devs
